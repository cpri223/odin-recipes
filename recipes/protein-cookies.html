Peanut Butter Protein Cookies

Description

These delightfully soft peanut butter cookies are packed with protein (7g to be exact!) and only require three ingredients!

Makes approximately 8. Serving size: 1 cookie.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup peanut butter (I like Jif creamy!)
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 6 scoops (138g) Orgain vanilla protein powder

Steps

  1. Set oven to 450deg.
  2. Combine all ingredients in a medium bowl.
  3. Separate dough mixture into 8 balls.
  4. Roll balls in granulated sugar (optional).
  5. Place balls onto a lined baking sheet, pressing down with your palm to slightly flatten the cookies.
  6. Bake for 5 minutes until bottom of cookies is golden.
