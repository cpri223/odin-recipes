Quinoa Peppers

Description

This delicious, healthy meal only takes about 20 minutes to make! Quinoa, brown rice, peppers, and zucchini topped with pesto and feta cheese make for a nutrient-packed meal. Keep it vegetarian or add some shredded rotisserie chicken for extra protein.

Makes approximately 5 large servings.

Ingredients

  • 2 10-oz. packs of Simple Truth Quinoa and Brown Rice
  • 3 bell peppers (any color)
  • 2 large or 3 small zucchini
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Feta cheese crumbles (as desired)
  • Pesto (as desired)
  • Shredded rotisserie chicken (optional)

Steps

  1. Set oven to 350 degrees. Line a baking sheet with aluminim foil and set aside.
  2. Slice peppers and zucchini.
  3. Spread vegetables on lined baking sheet and drizzle with avocado oil. Toss to coat vegetables evenly.
  4. Sprinkle vegetables with garlic powder, salt and pepper.
  5. Place baking sheet in oven and cook for 20 minutes.
  6. Cook quinoa and brown rice packs per instructions.
  7. Once cooked, combine vegetables with quinoa rice mix in a large bowl. Add chicken if desired.
  8. Serve with feta crumbles and pesto.
