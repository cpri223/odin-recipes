Easy Turkey Burgers

Description

These turkey burgers patties require only three ingredients and are packed with protein. Serve any way you want, or use my favorite toppings below.

Makes 6 large patties.

Ingredients

  • 2lb ground turkey meat (I use 93/7)
  • 1 cup italian breadcrumbs
  • 2 eggs
  • Italian seasoning
  • Lettuce (I like butter or romaine)
  • Cheese slices (colby pepper jack gives these a nice bite!)
  • Whole wheat or whole grain buns
  • Chick fil A sauce

Steps

  1. Set oven to 400 degrees. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Combine the meat, breadcrumbs, and eggs in a large bowl.
  3. Sprinkle in italian seasoning (as desired) and combine.
  4. Divide meat mixture into 6 large balls.
  5. Heat some oil in a large skillet on medium heat.
  6. Place 3 patties at a time in skillet, pressing slightly to flatten, and sear on all sides
  7. Place seared patties on baking sheet and baked for 10-15 minutes until fully cooked inside.
  8. Serve on buns with cheese, lettuce, and Chick fil A sauce
